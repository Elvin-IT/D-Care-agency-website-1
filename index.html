WHO WE ARE

D-Homecare Service is a service, dedicated to providing high-quality, personalized homecare services that support individuals in maintaining independence, dignity, and quality of life. We understand that home is more than just a place—it’s where comfort, familiarity, and peace of mind reside. Our team of experienced, compassionate caregivers is committed to delivering the support you or your loved ones need, right where it matters most.
Our aim is to enhance daily living through tailored care plans that respect each client’s unique needs, preferences, and routines. Whether it’s assistance with daily tasks, companionship, or specialized care, we are here to make life easier, safer, and more fulfilling for those we serve.

WHY US

Bridge to honouring dignity

We pride ourselves in delivering care with excellence especially to clients recovering or living with stroke and other ailments. With our dedicated team, we take a holistic approach to caring for your loved ones, offering access to a trusted team of Licensed Healthcare Professionals of Nurses who are well trained and carefully vetted to ensure the highest standard of service when and where it is needed most.

Our team also comprises of dedicated health care assistants who are well screened and trained, and are committed to delivering care of high quality ensuring dignity, respect, empathy and unwavering professionalism.

Our Services

Offer compassionate support across a wide range of services

Personal Care & Hygiene

Assistance with bathing, skin care, dressing, and grooming to promote comfort and well-being.

Meal Support

Preparation of nutritious meals and help with feeding, tailored to individual needs.

Medication Assistance

Support with oral and topical medications as prescribed by healthcare professionals.

Escorting services

Accompanying clients to hospital appointments, errands, or other outings for added peace of mind.

Companionship

Offering meaningful social interaction and emotional support to reduce isolation and enhance quality of care.

Nursing Care

Post Surgical care, Wound dressing, Observation checks, and general nursing homecare.

Light Housekeeping

Help with light household chores pertaining to the patient and as per our agreement
